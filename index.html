Shipping Agency

With vast experienced professionals, SAITRANS SHIPPING has the capacity to provide Ship Agency services and currently providing at Chennai, Ennore, Kattupalli, Tuticorin & Karaikal ports and expanding very soon to all Indian ports. We aim to provide cost-effective quality services, without compromising on key resources to Our Clients include Ship Owners, Operators, Managers/Charterers and Traders.

Our diligently trained and experienced personnel have the capacity to handle all operations swiftly and efficiently, thereby ensuring cost-effective services in the best interest of the client.

Apart from this, we understand the importance of interpersonal relations in this industry. Therefore, we are conscientious in maintaining regular and optimum relations with the Port and other Government Authorities. We work towards facilitating good relations between the overseas shipowners/traders and the local shippers/receivers. Our priority is to build strong and long-lasting relations with our Clients. We strive to keep our clients updated with all the relevant information regarding their shipments.

All essential information is given to the client before the arrival of the vessel and the client is also informed about the daily progress of the vessel until its departure. Thus, Customer Satisfaction is a priority to us.

We assure that we shall co-ordinate with the CHAs / Stevedore and as well as Logistical operators and other cargo related service providers to expedite the cargo operations so as to get the good turn-around of vessels calling under our agency enable the owners to fix further employment of the vessels as per the schedule. We shall also suggest the available cargos for back loading of the vessels calling under our agency which is most convenient to our principals by nominating us as their agents.

Ship Agency Services

  • Handling of Charterers Agency Matters
  • Handling of Owners Agency Matters
  • Statutory clearance of the vessels by the Customs, Port, Health, Immigration, Freight Tax Authorities
  • Monitoring of Vessel loading / un-loading operations by experienced supervisors
  • Owners Husbandry Matters
  • Handling of Crew Changes and repatriation formalities on time
  • Handling of Transit Ship Spares and delivery at onboard
  • Delivery CTM arrangements
  • Ship candling Services, Supply of Provisions, Ship Stores, Fresh Water, Bunkers etc.
  • Arrangement of Ship Repairing services

Most Important is to Provide most competitive PDA by maintaining quality of services. Also closely monitoring the costs incurred during vessels' Port stay so as to minimize expenses & endeavour that the costs are below the PDA amounts approved by vessel owners.
